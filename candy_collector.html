Candy Collector

8-bits to infinity VimJam game jam

Project Overview

Candy Collector is a game I created with a team of two programmers and two artists for the 8-bits to Infinity VimJam game jam. The theme for the game jam was "there and back," with a focus on collectables. Our concept was a platformer where players use collectables to progress through levels and reach the end. This project was completed in just one week.

I primarily worked on the platforming mechanics, including the collectables that assist the player in advancing through the levels. The short time frame and jam constraints made it a great experience for rapid prototyping and honing my gameplay development skills.
