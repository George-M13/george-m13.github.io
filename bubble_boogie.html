Bubble Boogie

Musical dance/rhythm VR game

Project Overview

Bubble Boogie is a VR rhythm/dance game where players follow choreographed routines by popping bubbles in time with the music. The game features a diverse selection of songs with varying difficulty levels, as well as a pro mode for an added challenge.

At Vstars Ltd, I worked as a Junior Game Programmer on Bubble Boogie, taking on a variety of responsibilities. I developed a local leaderboard system that tracks and displays players' best scores across all songs. I also worked on visual effects that change color dynamically based on the frequency bands of each song, adding to the immersion.

On the back end, I contributed to the JSON data that controls bubble timings for each track. This was a highly iterative process, requiring extensive playtesting to ensure that every note felt precise and responsive. I also worked hands-on in Unity to refine gameplay mechanics in VR, adjusting interaction responsiveness and player feedback to create a smoother and more intuitive experience. This role gave me valuable experience in both gameplay programming and data structuring while also deepening my understanding of developing for VR in Unity.

Key Features

  • Dynamic rhythm-based gameplay with adaptive difficulty
  • Music-reactive visual effects for an immersive experience
  • Precise VR interaction & scoring system
