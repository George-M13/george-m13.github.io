Immersify

A cutting-edge dental and nursing education application

Project Overview

Immersify is an innovative educational app offering interactive and immersive learning experiences, particularly in dental and nursing education. It provides a comprehensive platform that includes simulated clinical scenarios, interactive 3D models, multiple-choice questions, practice questions, and flashcards to enhance learning. Users can engage in augmented reality (AR) simulations that mimic real-life scenarios, allowing for practical application of theoretical knowledge.

I contributed to implementing the new 4.0 designs in the launcher, as well as in quizzes, flashcards, and various simulations. Additionally, I developed a "Style Sheet" tool for development in Unity, enabling seamless integration of both dark and light themes without duplication.

Currently I work as the XR Developer where in which I maintaining and updating the app, as well as developing new features with a focus on AR simulations.
